FAQ

Good to know

Does MaskMan need my X password?

No. MaskMan never sees or stores your password. You authorize access directly through X's official login screen, and you can revoke it anytime from your X settings.

How many tweets can I schedule?

The free plan includes up to 1,500 posts per month, matching X's free API tier. Higher tiers are available if you need more.

What happens if X is down when a post is due?

Poster retries automatically and notifies you if a scheduled post fails to send after several attempts.
